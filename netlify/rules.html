Contest Rules

These rules help keep GamerTagClips fair, competitive, and fun. By submitting a clip or voting, you agree to follow the rules below.

1. Eligibility

GamerTagClips competitions are intended for users who can legally participate in online contests and receive prizes in their local area. By entering, you confirm that you are responsible for complying with any local laws or restrictions that apply to you.

2. Submission Rules

You may only submit clips that you created, own, or have clear permission to share. Submitted clips must be public and viewable through the supported clip link on the site.

Do not submit stolen clips, impersonated content, misleading entries, duplicate spam submissions, or anything unlawful, abusive, hateful, pornographic, or otherwise inappropriate for the platform.

GamerTagClips reserves the right to remove or reject any submission at any time for any rule violation, quality concern, or suspicious activity.

3. Voting Rules

Voting is limited to one vote per person per week. Any attempt to bypass this limit through bots, scripts, fake accounts, incognito abuse, VPN abuse, automation, coordinated manipulation, or any other dishonest method is prohibited.

GamerTagClips may investigate suspicious voting patterns and may remove votes, remove entries, disqualify participants, or suspend voting activity whenever necessary to protect the integrity of the competition.

4. Winner Selection

The weekly winner is the eligible clip with the highest number of valid votes at the close of the competition window.

In the event of a tie, the clip that was submitted first ranks higher.

GamerTagClips may request reasonable verification before confirming a winner, including identity, ownership of the clip, or method of prize delivery.

5. Prize Terms

Any listed prize is subject to availability, verification, and final approval. GamerTagClips reserves the right to substitute, modify, delay, or cancel a prize if needed for fraud prevention, eligibility issues, platform abuse, technical issues, or other operational reasons.

If a selected winner does not respond in a reasonable timeframe, cannot be verified, or is found to have violated the rules, GamerTagClips may select another winner or choose not to award the prize.

6. Content Rights

You keep ownership of your original clip. By submitting it to GamerTagClips, you give GamerTagClips permission to display, promote, share, and feature your clip, gamertag, and entry details on the site and in related promotional content for the platform.

7. Platform Discretion

GamerTagClips reserves full discretion to review entries, review votes, remove suspicious activity, disqualify users, correct mistakes, pause a competition, modify contest structure, or cancel a contest if fairness, technical stability, or platform integrity is at risk.

All platform decisions regarding rules, abuse prevention, vote validity, eligibility, and winner selection are final.

8. Platform Disclaimer

GamerTagClips is an independent platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any game publisher, console manufacturer, streaming platform, or social media platform unless explicitly stated.

9. Acceptance of Rules

By submitting a clip, voting, or participating in any GamerTagClips competition, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to these rules.
